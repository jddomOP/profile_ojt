MEC Networks Corporation

Company Summary: MEC Networks Corporation is the premier Information and Communications Technology (ICT) distributor and solutions provider in the Philippines. Founded in 1995, the company has operated for over 30 years, playing a significant role in developing the country's ICT infrastructure by distributing global technology brands to the local market. The company is headquartered at the MEC Centre in Quezon City, National Capital Region, and maintains a major branch in Cebu City to serve the Visayas region. It is currently led by its President, Merrick Chua. Core Business and Products MEC functions primarily as a value-added distributor and B2B technology partner, bridging the gap between global tech manufacturers and local enterprises. The company offers end-to-end digital and infrastructure solutions across a variety of categories: Networking & Connectivity: Cabling, switching, routing, and wireless infrastructure. Cybersecurity & Surveillance: Enterprise security systems, threat assessment, and physical surveillance solutions. Communications: Unified communications and telephony systems tailored for modern digital workplaces. Data & Infrastructure: Application, storage, cloud computing, big data solutions, and power management.
